Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

At CodeSentinel AI, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your information when you use our code analysis service.

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your personal information and your code. We never sell your data to third parties.

GDPR Compliant SSL Encrypted Secure Storage

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Name: Your display name for personalization
  • Email Address: For account creation and communication
  • Authentication Data: Securely stored via Firebase Authentication

2. Code Analysis Data

  • Code Snippets: Temporarily processed for analysis (not permanently stored)
  • Analysis Results: Quality scores, bug counts, security issues
  • Analysis Metadata: Timestamps, language, file sizes

3. Usage Information

  • Activity Logs: Features used, analysis frequency
  • Device Information: Browser type, OS, screen resolution
  • IP Address: For security and analytics

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

Purpose Description
Service Delivery Provide code analysis and generate results
Account Management Create and maintain your account
Service Improvement Improve our AI models and algorithms
Communication Send updates, notifications, and support
Security Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
Analytics Understand usage patterns and trends

Data Storage & Security

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in Firebase with enterprise-grade security
  • Access Control: Strict access controls and authentication
  • Regular Backups: Automated backups for data protection

Code Privacy

Important: Your code snippets are processed in real-time for analysis and are NOT permanently stored on our servers. Only analysis metadata (scores, counts) is retained.

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and hosting
  • Google Gemini AI: AI-powered code analysis
  • GitHub API: Repository analysis (when you provide access)

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:

  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • GitHub Privacy Statement: docs.github.com/privacy

Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your analysis history
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@codesentinel.ai

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in
  • Preferences: Remember your settings
  • Analytics: Understand how you use our service

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect functionality.

Data Retention

Children's Privacy

CodeSentinel AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

  • Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance
